About

At ViaReach, our main goal is to help you and your loved ones. We provide services such as transportation, live-in assistance, home goods delivery and more to assure you're living comfortably and safely. We are trying to inspire others to perform random acts of kindness, planning events to help those less fortunate, and to just be better people. We are non-denominational, non-religious, and non-profit. We strive to help those who need it most by inspiring others to reach out. With our online services, checking in with those you care about has never been more convenient, and neither has living with ease.

"My motivation to help those who are disabled stems from when I was disabled. After being in a car accident that left me paralyzed temporarily, it opened my eyes to the different treatment I was recieving. I experienced how difficult it is to not be able to walk. From that moment on I did not want anyone else to have to feel left out like I was. With ViaReach, we hope to provide opportunities that otherwise would be unobtainable to people living with disabilities every day."

- Yamilex Godinez
