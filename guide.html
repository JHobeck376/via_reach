Read this for links locations, future developments, and intended purposes of the webpage.

Links

Forms

  • To find the form where customers would be able to request custom services, click on the "login" button. Existing users would be able to request custom services on a forum similar to Craigslist or Yahoo! Answers.

  • To access the form where users would be able to create an account click on the box titled "Sign Up" on the home page. Users would be able to create a username and password to acess their custom analysis of services we could provide.

  • To access the form where non-users or account holders would be able to sign up to volunteer, click on the box titled "Help Out" on the home page. This is where volunteers could apply for jobs or to assist individuals through the Service Request forum.

General Requirenments

  • Home, Story (About Page), Volunteer, (see above) and Contact pages are available at all time at the top of the page in the navigation bar. ViaReach services are explained on the Home page and Story page.

Pages

End Screens

  • The end screen of the Sign Up page, when in affect, will showcase customized service recomendation based off the the answers from the client. For example, if the form returned a value of "true" for "Do you need a wheelchair?" then a Taxi service or otherwise similar 3rd-party brand partership would be shown. A concept is that ViaReach would be able to give special discount to users.
